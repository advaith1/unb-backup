UnbelievaBoat - Donate


UnbelievaBoat is supported entirely by donations from the Discord community.

By donating, you'll receive perks based on your contribution.


Donation perk levels

£2+ GBP  [$2+ USD] You will receive the Donator role in the official server.
£5+ GBP  [$7+ USD] You'll be able to add the donator only version of the bot, this has music commands and better performance & uptime.
Perks of the donator only bot include:
  • High quality music and radio functionality.
    Play music or streams from YouTube, Bandcamp, SoundCloud, Twitch, Vimeo, Mixer, other music URLs
  • Better performance.
  • Better uptime.
£10+ GBP  [$14+ USD] You will receive the Super Donator role in the official server.

Receiving your perks for donating

To receive your perks the donation has to be linked to your Discord account.
To do this use the verify-donation command with the transaction ID in the bots DMs (direct messages).
Your transaction ID can be found in the email confirmation from PayPal, or payment history.
Example: !verify-donation 36C38912MN9658832
